Why I Started Fyzo

As both a creator and community member, I've seen how difficult it can be for creators to feel heard on the platforms they depend on.

I started Fyzo because I believe creators and users deserve a stronger voice in the communities they help build.

This isn't about replacing every platform overnight.

It's about creating something that values transparency, communication, and long-term trust.

We're starting small, listening carefully, and building alongside the community from day one.

We're working closely with creators to gather feedback, understand their needs, and build a platform that serves both creators and their communities.
